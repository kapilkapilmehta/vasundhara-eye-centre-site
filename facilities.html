Home · Facilities

Facilities

A compact, purpose-built clinic with a dedicated operation theatre, diagnostic suite and comfortable waiting area — all on a single floor.

Operation Theatre

Our OT is designed around small-incision eye surgery — compact, sterile and equipped for day-care procedures.

  • Zeiss operating microscope with assistant scope
  • Phacoemulsification unit with dual-function handpiece
  • Continuous vital-signs monitoring during surgery
  • Dedicated sterilisation with autoclave & ETO
  • Emergency oxygen and resuscitation readiness

Diagnostic Suite

Most common investigations happen right at the clinic — cutting down travel and keeping care coordinated.

  • Autorefractor & keratometer
  • Slit-lamp biomicroscope (photo-documentation capable)
  • Applanation tonometer
  • Direct & indirect ophthalmoscope
  • A-scan biometry for IOL power calculation
  • Visual field & OCT — via partner labs when indicated

Patient Experience

We've kept the clinic on a single floor to make access easy for elderly patients, people using walking aids and those with visual difficulty.

  • Single-level access from the ground floor
  • Comfortable waiting lounge
  • Pharmacy and optical dispensing next door
  • Two-wheeler and car parking on Ramphal Chowk
  • Free drinking water, washroom access

Standards & certifications

The clinic holds the necessary operating licenses and has been assessed by quality bodies. Certificates are available on request at the reception.

QCI Certified

Accredited by the Quality Council of India for clinic standards.

DPCC Authorised

Cleared by the Delhi Pollution Control Committee for biomedical waste handling.

ROHINI Registered

On the Registry of Hospitals in Network of Insurance — enabling cashless claims.

CGHS & ECHS

Empanelled for cashless services under central and ex-servicemen's health schemes.
