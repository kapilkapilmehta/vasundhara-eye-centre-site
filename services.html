Our services

Every patient journey begins with a careful consultation. From there, we offer a full range of diagnostic and surgical services — drawing on in-house expertise and visiting consultants for subspecialties.

Cataract Surgery

Cataract surgery is our highest-volume procedure. We perform phacoemulsification — a small-incision, stitchless technique — using modern equipment in a dedicated, fully sterile operation theatre.

  • Pre-operative biometry and IOL power calculation
  • Foldable monofocal, toric and multifocal IOLs
  • Day-care procedure, typical in-and-out time 2–3 hours
  • Structured follow-up protocol

OPD & Diagnostics

A thorough outpatient examination is the foundation of everything else we do. Our OPD is equipped with the instruments needed for a complete workup in a single visit.

  • Refraction and vision assessment
  • Slit-lamp biomicroscopy
  • Applanation tonometry (glaucoma screening)
  • Fundus examination & imaging
  • Autorefractor, keratometer & A-scan biometry

Full service list

All listed services are available at the clinic, or through clearly-explained referral pathways to our partner hospitals.

Cataract Surgery

Phacoemulsification with foldable monofocal, toric and multifocal IOLs.

LASIK Evaluation

Pre-op counselling and candidate assessment; refractive procedures performed at partner centres.

Oculoplastic Surgery

Eyelid (ptosis, entropion, ectropion), lacrimal drainage and orbital surgery.

Glaucoma Management

Tonometry, gonioscopy, disc assessment; medical and surgical management.

Retina Services

Fundoscopy, diabetic retinopathy screening; subspecialty referral where indicated.

Refraction & Spectacles

Comprehensive refraction, prescription glasses and contact lens fitting.

Minor OT Procedures

Chalazion incision & curettage, foreign-body removal, lid cyst excision.

Pediatric Ophthalmology

Vision screening, squint evaluation and amblyopia management.

Medical Board & Fitness

Eye certificates for driving, employment, passport and medical boards.
