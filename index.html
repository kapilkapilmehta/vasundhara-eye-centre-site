About the clinic

Specialist care, delivered with humility.

Vasundhara Eye Centre was founded in 2008 by Dr. Anjali Mehta — an Ex-Army eye surgeon trained at AFMC, Pune; a Médecins Sans Frontières alumna; and a graduate of the Diploma in Tropical Medicine & Hygiene from London. Three decades of practice across military hospitals, MSF deployments and private clinics inform the unhurried, evidence-based care we bring to every patient.

Forty percent of Dr. Mehta's time goes to community care through the Sagar Trust — over 20,000 underserved patients have been reached through free outreach camps.
