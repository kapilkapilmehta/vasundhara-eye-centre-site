How cashless works, step by step

1. Carry your documents

Insurance card, policy document, a government photo ID, and any prior prescriptions.

2. Consultation & plan

Dr. Mehta will evaluate and recommend a plan. We'll give you the estimated cost in writing.

3. Pre-authorisation

We submit the pre-auth request to your insurer/TPA. Most approvals come in 24–48 hours.

4. Procedure & claim

Once approved, the procedure is done. We handle the final claim with your insurer directly.
